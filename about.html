I am proficient in Java and have experience in C++, HTML, PHP, SQL and C#. I ended up choosing programming because after taking some of the beginning programming and networking courses at Penn State I found that I enjoyed programming far more. Even though I am not going into web design I decided to take this course because I thought it would be intersting and make me a more well rounded programmer, since until I took this class the majority of my experience was in C++ and Java, with only a little bit of experience with the other languages.
